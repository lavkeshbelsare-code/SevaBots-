Cleaning Robotics Solutions

SevaBots currently focuses exclusively on precision cleaning robots. We offer three distinct series engineered for different scales of operation.

H-Series

Home Cleaning Robots

  • Designed for houses and small residential spaces
  • Compact, whisper-quiet, and highly intuitive
  • Solar-assisted charging dock for energy efficiency
  • Optimized for flawless daily cleaning

S-Series

Startup & Small Biz

  • Perfect for small offices, cafes, and modern startups
  • Higher dirt/liquid capacity than the H-Series
  • Smart room navigation & dynamic obstacle detection
  • Enhanced solar support for extended working hours
